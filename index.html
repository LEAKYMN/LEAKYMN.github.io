About Me

My name is Leakey Mokaya N. and I am a Web/Mobile App Developer, Software Engineer, Front-end Developer, UX&UI Designer and Graphic Designer, currently living in Nairobi, Kenya. I have a Bachelor of Science in Applied Computer and Technology from United States International University, and my primary focus and inspiration for my studies is Web Development.

In my free time, I study human computer interface and the psychology of human computer interaction. I am both driven and self-motivated, and I am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques. I am very passionate about Web Development, and strive to better myself as a developer, and the development community as a whole.
